Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 23, 2025 | Version 1.0

Flare Food ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Health Data: Meal information, symptoms, fluid intake, caffeine consumption
  • Profile Information: Name, dietary preferences, health goals
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • HealthKit Data: With your permission, we sync caffeine and water intake data

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve our services:

  • Track and analyze your dietary habits and symptoms
  • Identify correlations between foods and symptoms
  • Generate personalized insights and reports
  • Sync data with Apple HealthKit (when authorized)

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure frameworks. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • On-device encryption for all personal data
  • Optional biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
  • No data transmission to external servers
  • Secure iCloud backup (if enabled on your device)

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app
  • Export: Export your data in CSV or JSON format
  • Delete: Delete any or all of your data at any time
  • HealthKit: Manage HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings

Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with any third parties. The app integrates with:

  • Apple HealthKit: Only when you explicitly grant permission
  • iCloud: For device backup (controlled by your iOS settings)

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" and version number.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support section.

By using Flare Food, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
